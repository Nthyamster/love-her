I’m Sorry

I’m sorry,
I wasn’t enough.
I’m sorry I cared too much,
wanted too much,
loved you in ways
you never asked for.

I’m sorry for the warmth
in my hugs,
for needing you
in a world filled with lies.
I never wanted to use you,
I just wanted us to last.

Now I see,
I was the only one
holding on,
while you let go
long before
you ever told me.

I’m sorry.
But more than that,
I’m sorry you felt
I never loved you.
I tried my best,
but it wasn’t good enough.
